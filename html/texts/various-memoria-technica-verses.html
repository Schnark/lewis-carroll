Date of Easter

List my song to!
’Tis as wrong to
Save a flea
As rob a bee.

Kings and Queens of England

Crazy belief we cause to none:
A fact, if dead of a hilly run.

Execution of Charles

We pardon faults like thine,
Whose ending was so fine.

Cromwell made Protector

Ambition was thy fault:
Thine own self to exalt.

Years of enthronisations (all consonants):

Anne: 1702, Victoria: 1837

Foundation of Colleges

Brasenose College

With a nose that is brazen
Our gate we emblazon.

Christ Church

Ring Tom when you please:
We ask but small fees.

St John’s College

They must have a bevel
To keep them so level.

Brasenose College: 1509, Christ Church: 1546, St John’s College: 1555

Other Dates

Battle of Crécy

The string is wet! If so,
Useless is my bow!

Wycliffe’s Bible completed

The Bible to translate
Brought Wycliffe nought but hate.

Battle of Crécy: 1346 (first line), Wycliffe’s Bible completed: 1383
