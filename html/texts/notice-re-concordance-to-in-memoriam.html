Notice re Concordance to ‘In Memoriam’

Source: probably printed 1881, also added to advertisements

This was published in 1862, and is adapted to all editions in which the Poem consists of 130 sections. About the year 1870, a new section was introduced as No. 39; for all subsequent editions, the Index may be used equally well, by simply adding “1” to every sectional number above “38.”
