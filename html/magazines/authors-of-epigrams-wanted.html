The (almost really) Complete Works of Lewis Carroll

Authors of Epigrams Wanted

Source: Notes and Queries, July 27, 1889

Answered September 7, 1889, https://academic.oup.com/nq/article/s7-VIII/193/193/4441108

Can any of your readers give me the names of the authors of the following epigrams?—

Si placeat brevitas, hoc breve carmen habe.

“Femina dux facti: facti dux femina—” “Quid tum?” “Quid tum? Tum facti femina dux fuit.” “O!”

C. L. Dodgson
