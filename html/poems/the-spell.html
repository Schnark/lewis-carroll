Source: The Walking-Stick of Destiny (extracted, from image)

From a window thrown,
To the ground he fell,
Yet he broke no bone,
He’s alive and well!
And the poisoned sip
Of the offered cup,
Hath missed his lip,
He hath ta’en no sup!
Yet, Signor, beware,
Or thou shalt rue!
There is much to dare,
There is much to do!
