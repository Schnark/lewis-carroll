My First has no beard

Source: written for Alexandra Kitchin, perhaps around 1880

My First has no beard—but its whiskers abound:
My Next has a beard—but no eyes:
My whole has two eyes—and a nourishing sound
That reminds one of puddings & pies.

Lewis Carroll (written for Xie)

Solution: A kit, chin: A. Kitchin
