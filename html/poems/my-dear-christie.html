My dear Christie

Source: sent to Sophia Christina “Christie” Taylor, October 1869?

My dear Christie,
I greatly fear
I’m wanted here,
Which makes it clear
I can’t appear
At your “pour rire”—
Would I were freer!
So, with a tear
(At which don’t sneer),
I am, my dear,
Your most sincere
C. L. Dodgson
