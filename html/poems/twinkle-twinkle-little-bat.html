Twinkle, twinkle, little bat!

Source: Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland (extracted, connected)

Parody on The Star by Jane Taylor

Twinkle, twinkle, little bat!
How I wonder what you’re at!
Up above the world you fly,
Like a teatray in the sky.
Twinkle, twinkle——
