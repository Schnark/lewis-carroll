Test-Paper. Arithmetic. [1]

Source: probably printed 1863, authorship not entirely certain

  1. Find the G. C. M. and L. C. M. of 12, 16, 18.
  2. Simplify 12+32+12+23+5713+37.
  3. Define Factor, Multiple, Decimal Fraction. Give the rule (1) for placing the point in division of decimals; (2) for reducing a circulating decimal to a vulgar fraction.
  4. Reduce 29 of 16 of a guinea to the fraction of 710 of 13 of 1l. 4s.
  5. Find the cost of painting the walls of a room 24 ft. 3 in. long, 15 ft. 9 in. wide, and 12 ft. 6 in. high, at 3s. 4d. a square foot.
  6. Find the square root of 40028.00490.
  7. If 10 men mow 9 acres in 24 days, how many men will be required to mow 7 times as many acres in one-fifth of the time?
  8. Find the difference between the discount allowed on 918l., paid 4 years before it is due (interest taken at 5 per cent.), and the interest charged on the same sum for the same time.
  9. A man sells a horse for 41l. 16s., thus gaining 412 per cent. on the purchase-money. What did he give for it?
  10. Divide 1300l. into 3 parts in the ratio of 12, 13, 14.

[Christ Church]
